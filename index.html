Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We’re here to help!

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of delivery and logistics services, including: Same-day, next-day, and scheduled deliveries Local, national, and international shipping. Secure storage and warehousing solutions. Freight and cargo transport. Specialized packaging for fragile or high-value items and Custom logistics solutions for businesse

How do I book a delivery?

Booking a delivery is simple! You can book directly through our website by selecting your service type, entering your shipment details, and scheduling a pickup. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team to assist with booking.

What is the process for shipping an item internationally?

To ship internationally, you’ll need to provide the destination address and shipment details. We handle customs clearance, shipping documentation, and ensure your goods are delivered securely and on time. Our team will guide you through the necessary steps to make international shipping hassle-free.

How can I track my shipment?

Once your shipment is on its way, you will receive a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our website to monitor your package's status in real-time.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in transparency. The price you see during booking is the final cost, with no hidden charges. If there are any additional fees for special services, they will be clearly outlined before you complete your booking.
